Employee Monitoring Consent Form

Please read and sign below to acknowledge

Important: This form must be signed before the monitoring software is installed on your system.
What is being monitored:
Screenshots: Automatic screenshots will be captured every 30 minutes during work hours.
Activity Tracking: Mouse movements and keyboard activity will be recorded to track work engagement.
Idle Time: System idle time will be monitored to ensure productivity.
Active Window: Currently active application window will be recorded.
Data Usage:
  • Data will be used solely for productivity and performance monitoring
  • Data will be stored securely on company servers
  • Only authorized management personnel will have access
  • Data will not be shared with third parties
Employee Rights:
  • You may request access to your collected data
  • You may request deletion of your data upon employment termination
  • You may opt-out by contacting HR (subject to company policy)
